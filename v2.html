Section 05 — Before you come

Questions

What's included in the price?

Track entry fees for your booked laps, the first tank of fuel, and the fully prepared car with its race setup. Extra laps taken on the day are added when you return the car.

What happens if I crash, or damage the track?

Basic insurance is included and caps your liability at €14,000 excess. Extra insurance (+€99) brings that down to €10,000. Premium insurance (+€199) brings it to €7,000 and adds full cover for Armco barrier damage and track towing — the two costs that hurt most at the Nürburgring.

Can I drive during public Touristenfahrten?

Yes. Despite being a 100% VLN-class racing car, it's fully road legal, so you can run it during public Touristenfahrten sessions as well as on trackdays.

What are the driver requirements?

Minimum age 21, plus a valid EU or international driver's licence. No racing licence needed. If it's your first time on the Nordschleife, book the co-pilot — €49 per lap and worth every cent.

Do you run trackdays?

Yes — a flat €349 per day including 5 laps, with extra laps at €70 each.
